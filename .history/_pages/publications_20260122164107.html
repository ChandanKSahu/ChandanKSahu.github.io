ReqNet and ReqSim: A Network and Semantic Similarity Dataset of Requirements from the Tree Structure of System Requirement Specifications

ASME Journal of Computing and Information Science in Engineering (JCISE)
Requirements Engineering Deep Learning NLP Semantic Similarity

Problem: Lack of structured datasets for training ML models on requirements engineering tasks, hindering traceability and change propagation analysis.

Solution: Fine-tuned Sentence-BERT for requirements domain; created requirement networks using embeddings and graph theory for traceability.

Novelty: First publicly available dataset of requirement networks and semantic similarity scores extracted from system requirement specification documents.

Machine Learning Based Prediction of Melt Pool Morphology in a Laser-Based Powder Bed Fusion Additive Manufacturing Process

International Journal of Production Research (IJPR)
Additive Manufacturing LSTM cGAN Melt Pool Morphology

Problem: Predicting melt pool size and shape in laser powder bed fusion is critical for quality but challenging due to complex physics.

Solution: LSTM network predicting melt pool size (90.7% accuracy) and cGAN synthesizing melt pool shape (SSIM: 0.91).

Novelty: First combined approach using LSTM for size prediction and conditional GAN for morphology synthesis in LPBF process.

PDF DOI
DOI: 10.1080/00207543.2023.2201860
